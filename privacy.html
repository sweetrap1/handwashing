Privacy Policy for Handwash Tracking

Last Updated: June 8, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of your information when You use the Handwash Tracking application (the "App").

Information We Collect

The Handwash Tracking app is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect and store the following information directly on your device:

  • Employee Information: Employee number, name, and department as entered by the administrator.
  • Handwash Logs: The date, time, and employee number for each handwash performed.

All of this information is stored locally on the device and is not transmitted, shared, or uploaded to any server.

Camera Usage

The App requests permission to use the device's front-facing camera for one specific purpose:

  • Motion Detection: To confirm that a user is present at the end of the handwashing timer, the app uses the camera to detect motion. No pictures or videos are ever recorded, stored, or transmitted by the app. The camera feed is processed in real-time for motion and then immediately discarded.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information stored within the app on your device. However, no electronic storage method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update Our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify You of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
